Our Story

Rumesh Guest was founded in 2010 with a passion for authentic cuisine and a love for bringing people together. Our mission is to serve delicious, homemade dishes in a warm and welcoming atmosphere.

We believe in using only the freshest ingredients, traditional recipes, and a touch of creativity to make every meal memorable. Whether you're here for a family dinner, a romantic date, or a celebration, we promise an experience you'll cherish.
